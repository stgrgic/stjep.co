Introduction

My name is Stjepan Grgic. I describe myself as a designer who loves to code, take photos, draw... Interaction design and user experience are the fields I focus most of my energy lately. There, I can be creative with everything I love to do.
Drawing helps me visualize my thoughts and express my ideas. Photography was essential for my understanding of light, color and all principles of great design.
Coding gives me the power to prototype interactions and to build anything I can imagine.
Currently, I am living in Zagreb, Croatia where I run my own UI/UX design studio called underlayer.
