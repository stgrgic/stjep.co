How it works?

Agrivi farm management software lets you plan, monitor and analyze all activities on your farms easily. Tillage, planting, spraying, fertilization, irrigation, harvesting and all other activities are managed with a few clicks.

Powerful farm analytics lets you make the right decisions at the right time. Say no to gut-feeling decision making and welcome data-driven farm management.

Who are you?

Whether you are small or large farmer, agricultural cooperative or large enterprise company looking for a tailor-made solution, we have a solution for you.
